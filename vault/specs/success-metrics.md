# Success Metrics

## Judging Scorecard

| Criteria | Weight | Strategy |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Innovation & Originality | Equal | Novel AI approach, not a wrapper around ChatGPT |
| Technical Complexity & Execution | Equal | Clean code, working demo, solid architecture |
| Product Experience & Design | Equal | Polished UI, good UX, clear information hierarchy |
| Impact & Practical Value | Equal | Real sustainability problem, measurable outcomes |

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Working demo accessible online
- [ ] Clear sustainability impact story
- [ ] Clean, well-organized codebase
- [ ] Submission uploaded before Mar 15 @ 8:30am EDT

## Demo Criteria

- [ ] Can explain the problem in 1 sentence
- [ ] Live demo works end-to-end
- [ ] Shows AI doing something non-trivial
- [ ] Quantifiable or visible sustainability impact
- [ ] Looks polished (not raw terminal output)
